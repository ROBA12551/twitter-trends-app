Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does it take to respond to reports?

We typically review reports within 24 hours. Depending on the severity and complexity, additional time may be needed.

Q: Is my personal information safe?

Yes, your personal information is only used to respond to your report and is not shared with third parties. It is transmitted securely via Discord Webhook.

Q: Can I report anonymously?

Yes. You can submit a report without providing your email address or name if you prefer to remain anonymous.

Q: Can I upload multiple images?

Yes, you can select and upload multiple images. Each file must be 5MB or smaller.

Q: What types of content can I report?

You can report inappropriate content, copyright violations, spam, illegal content, bugs, or other issues. Select the appropriate category in the form.

Q: What happens after I submit a report?

Our moderation team will review your report and take appropriate action, which may include removing content, warning users, or reporting to authorities if necessary.
